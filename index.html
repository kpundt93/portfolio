Why Epicodus?

About six months before COVID-19 changed the world, I decided that I wanted to make a career change and started exploring options in the tech world. I found my way to development and realized that with the right skills, I could make a career out of solving puzzles and helping businesses solve problems. Enter Epicodus. I chose Epicodus because I felt that they had the program I needed to refine my existing skills, learn some new ones, and get me ready to make the career switch into the tech world.
